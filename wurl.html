The WURL

Wasatch Ultimate Ridge Linkup

What is the WURL? Well according to Jared Campbell, its creator, the WURL is an incredible ridge route circumnavigating Little Cottonwood Canyon in the Wasatch Mountains of Utah. The route mainly consists of scrambling, boulder hopping, and a few rugged trails. It is ~36 miles long with ~18kft of elevation gain.

Below is a list of some things that it contains.

  1. Climbing
    • Jared may have said that this was a scrambling route, but some sections are very much 5th class.
    • Monte Cristo and Devils Castle are said to be the most technical peaks.
  2. A Full Day
    • To complete this in 24 hours is considered a fast time.
    • The majority of people take closer to 30 hours.
    • This means you are going to spend a full night on the trail!
  3. Suffering
    • Everyone I know that has completed it has told me of the all time lows they experience.
    • Some even reach the point of hallucinations.
    • Fun!
